# The Corsair's Logbook

I always like to write about the technologies I test/use as it gives me the opportunity of writing about "a thing" in my own way, telling a story.
That's how personally, I learn best. I mean, I look at a blog post and see the chapters, maybe I already know some of them and move ahead or tell myself "is there a detail I missed last time I read"?

So the question is: how does other relate to my technical blog posts? Do they relate to the story? Did they learn something?

While I can't be in the heads of the readers, I definitively can convey my own side of the story and what, as the Captain Corsair, I went through ... but not only.

So welcome to this more personal side of the Corsair, and if you found this Logbook, let me know on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/nunixtech) what you thought.

The Captain Corsair
