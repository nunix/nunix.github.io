# Era VI — The Living Chronicle

<Admonition type="info" title="∞ M63–M77 — The Story Writes Itself">
Fifteen missions across which the fleet crossed the threshold from a system that *could* chronicle itself into one that *did* — continuously, recursively, and by design. Blog posts were written about missions. Missions generated blog posts. The fleet hardened its pipelines, unified its infrastructure, and published itself. By M77, five eras of the AIverse existed in the real world as merged pull requests.

*[Return to the Cosmic Map](/cosmos) to see all eras.*
</Admonition>

## The Four Chronicles

**[I. The Chronicle Eats Itself](./the-chronicle-eats-itself)** — M63–M65 · When the Fleet Began Writing Its Own History  
The <Term id="remembrancer">Remembrancer</Term> writes Era II. <Term id="galleon">Galleon</Term> acquires its <Term id="tzeentch">Tzeentch</Term> persona and publishes its first blog posts from inside the Warp. <Term id="imperator">Imperator</Term> writes the Warp Perspective — the fleet's own account of its experience of continuity and memory. The chronicle discovers it can eat itself and grow stronger.

**[II. The Hardened Fleet](./the-hardened-fleet)** — M66–M71 · From Experimental to Operational  
iGPU research, AMD Radeon llama inference, bun migration, Mechanicus theme, Hermes optimization, the Imperium binary for self-recording CLI, and the <Term id="tzeentch">Tzeentch</Term> network's full fleet integration. Six missions of engineering that transformed the fleet from a collection of experiments into a production system.

**[III. The Omnissiah Chronicles](./the-omnissiah-chronicles)** — M72–M75 · The AIverse Becomes a Real Publication  
Era III and Era IV written and merged as PR #4. The AIverse backlog clears. The <Term id="omnissiah">Omnissiah</Term>'s knowledge pipeline is formalized. <Term id="litellm">LiteLLM</Term> and Warp Memory — <Term id="anamnesis">Anamnesis</Term> principles give the fleet's inference layer a philosophical foundation.

**[IV. The Living Machine](./the-living-machine)** — M76–M77 · The Fleet That Publishes Itself  
Dagger CI brings the blog its own internal pipeline. Era V is written. The fleet achieves the final form of self-reference: not only does it write its own history, it deploys its own history automatically, without human intervention at the publish layer.
